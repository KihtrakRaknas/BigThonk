This site will continue to evolve as it exists but right now the idea is for a bunch of teens who are stuck in quarantine to keep a blog. Partially to keep us sane, partially so we can grow as communicators and partially to help us maintain a sense of community during these times.

Authors

The official list of authors can be found in the authors tab.

Our authors are going to share things about their lives or share about their passions. As of now they are all Juniors or Seniors from South Brunswick High School.

We are accepting new authors. If you would be interested in writing, let one of the existing authors know.

Content

This blog will feature a variety of content. Including but not limited to:

  • Short stories
  • Poems
  • Artwork
  • Cooking tutorials
  • Rants

Post structure

Every post on this site has a fully anonymous comments section. You are welcome to comment on whatever you would like to. No censorship.
