A Lack of Clarity is determined to provide a home for experimental and unique literary pieces in varying mediums for young writers. We publish works of various genres, styles, and lengths that leave the reader thinking.

Authors

The official list of authors can be found in the authors tab.

Our authors are all young, emerging writers looking to bring something unique, meaningful, and lasting to the literary world.

We are currently accepting new authors. If you are interested in writing, please message us through Instagram.

We are also accepting submissions. As of right now, we do offer feedback on every submission we receive. We want to give every submission a good read, so wait time can be anywhere from one to two weeks. Message us through Instagram for further details.

Content

This blog will feature a variety of content. Including but not limited to:

  • Fiction
  • Poetry
  • Nonfiction
  • Visual art
  • Reviews
  • Translations

Post Structure

Every post on this site has a fully anonymous comments section. You are welcome to comment on whatever you would like to. No censorship. However, we do ask for the courtesy of our readers to refrain from using offensive language (slurs, derogatory terms, etc.).
