Center

Swayam Chakraborty

4/29/2020 8:46:09 PM


Orbit
You walk the center
The field of play
You make a circle
You mark your center
You start your game
The Game of Life

With
Your center marked
You start to play

Time as the field
The play quintessentially life
You don’t decide
Your team or group
Quite literally throwing you for a loop
How do you win?
You don’t
You Don’t
Life will go on
And you will play
Your center marked
You begin again
Life will go on
All you have is gain
Depressing?
Maybe
Saddening?
Maybe
Fulfilling?
Yes

Your buzzer may have started but a time out is called
Your birthday is here
Pure bliss
A moment in time
Framed
Your center marked
You begin again
The game is played
And everything is lost
For everything is nothing
And nothing is to blame
Center
Your center is marked

Anytime you feel bad
Insignificant
Worthless
Lonely
Remember
Remember the center
The center of your game
The center of your time
Your birth
If the universe is endless
Then the center is everywhere
You are
You are your center
You are the center of the world
Of the universe
You are
Your game
Its being played
Remember to live life
Remember to win your game

You are
You are the center
You are the winner
As your time slows
As your last time out is called
You walk the center
The center marked
And you smile
